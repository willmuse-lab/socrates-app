## The one-liner
**Socrates** helps teachers redesign their assignments so AI can't do the work for students — without banning AI.

**Tagline:** *Wisdom in the Age of Artificial Intelligence*

## What it does
A teacher pastes (or uploads) an assignment. Socrates:
1. Scores how "AI-resilient" it is (0–100).
2. Breaks down exactly how a student could use AI to shortcut it.
3. Offers three one-click redesigns — **Bronze** (quick tweak), **Silver** (real restructure), **Gold** (transformational) — each rewritten and ready to hand out.
4. Auto-generates **differentiated versions** for IEP/504, English-language learners, and gifted students.
5. Exports to PDF, Word, or Google Docs, and saves to a personal library.

Every suggestion is grounded in published research on AI and assessment.

## Who it's for
K–12 and college teachers, department heads, and school/district administrators who are anxious about AI-assisted cheating but don't want to police it. Primary buyer personas: the individual teacher, the department, the school, the district.

## The core belief (the "why")
Banning AI is impossible and counterproductive. Instead of fighting AI, Socrates helps teachers design assignments where AI *can't replace genuine student thinking* — turning AI from the "bad guy" into a learning tool.

---

## Proof points
- **Research-backed:** built on the Triple-A framework, Process-Product assessment (Awadallah Alkouk & Khlaif, 2024), PAIRR (Sperber et al., 2025), UNESCO 2023 guidance, Bloom's, UDL, and more.
- **Differentiation built in:** every redesign auto-adapts for IEP, ELL, and gifted learners.
- **Fast:** paste an assignment, get a scored redesign in under a minute.

## Brand look & feel
- **Logo:** a minimalist owl (wisdom), teal-to-green gradient.
- **Voice:** calm, credible, teacher-to-teacher. Not hypey. "We get it — teaching is hard." Anti-fear, pro-empowerment.
- **Typography:** Inter (clean sans-serif) for body; Georgia *italic* for elegant headlines.
- **Color palette (from the app):**
  - Background warm off-white `#F9F9F7`
  - Near-black text `#1A1A1A`
  - Coral accent `#FF6B6B`
  - Success green `#6BCB77`, warning yellow `#FFD93D`, info blue `#4D96FF`
- **Note on consistency:** the owl logo is teal/green while the app's accent is coral `#FF6B6B`. You may want to pick one accent direction before producing a lot of collateral.
